Our Methods

Hot water extraction — also known as steam cleaning — is one of the most effective methods for deep-cleaning carpets. It works by spraying hot water mixed with a cleaning solution deep into the carpet fibers. The heat loosens dirt, oils, allergens, and stains, while a powerful vacuum immediately extracts the water along with all the debris.

This method:

  • Penetrates deeper than surface-level cleaning
  • Kills bacteria and dust mites
  • Leaves your carpet clean, fresh, and residue-free

At TrueBlue Carpet Cleaning, we use professional-grade equipment and eco-friendly solutions to ensure a deep clean that’s safe for kids, pets, and the environment.

Cold water extraction is a gentle carpet cleaning method that uses cool water and cleaning agents to flush out dirt, dust, and allergens from your carpet. Unlike hot water extraction, it doesn’t rely on heat — making it ideal for delicate fibers, natural materials, or situations where high heat isn't recommended.

Benefits of cold water extraction:

  • Safe for wool, silk, and heat-sensitive materials
  • Reduces risk of carpet shrinkage or color fading
  • Eco-conscious option with less energy use

At TrueBlue Carpet Cleaning, we use cold water extraction for specific fabrics and conditions where a gentler touch is needed — always tailoring the process to what’s safest and most effective for your carpet.

Dry carpet cleaning uses minimal moisture and specialized cleaning compounds to lift dirt and stains from your carpet. It’s a fast-drying, low-moisture method — perfect when you need quick results or have delicate flooring.

We ensure every cleaning method is safe for your carpet’s material. Before any service, we assess your carpet type to choose the best treatment for lasting results without damage.
